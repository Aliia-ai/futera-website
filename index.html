About Futera

Science at the core.
People at the heart.

We believe the most meaningful innovations happen where disciplines converge. Futera brings together expertise in neuroscience, biochemistry, artificial intelligence, and education to create products and platforms that genuinely improve how people learn, grow, and thrive.

Based in Luxembourg and building for the world, we conduct applied research and develop evidence-based methodologies at the intersection of cognitive science and technology — translating complex findings into accessible, impactful solutions with a particular focus on children's development and cognitive wellbeing.

Focus Areas

Where we build

Our projects live at the intersection of three powerful domains — each amplifying the others.

Education Technology

Reimagining learning for the children who need it most. We develop adaptive educational tools rooted in cognitive science — designed for gifted, neurodiverse, and twice-exceptional learners.

Health Technology

Bridging neuroscience and everyday wellness. We explore how AI and data-driven insights can support cognitive development, functional health, and proactive approaches to wellbeing.

AI & Neuroscience

The connective tissue across everything we build. We apply artificial intelligence to translate complex neuroscientific research into practical, scalable solutions for real-world impact.

Our Approach

Multidisciplinary
by design

The most overlooked opportunities exist between fields. Futera was founded on the conviction that breakthrough solutions emerge when you bring together perspectives that don't usually sit at the same table.

We are building a network of collaborators across neuroscience, education, AI, and functional medicine — because the future of human potential is inherently interdisciplinary.

01

Science-First

Every product begins with peer-reviewed research and clinical evidence. We develop proprietary methodologies and seek academic partnerships to ensure our solutions are grounded in rigorous science.

02

Human-Centered

Technology serves people, not the other way around. We design for real families, real learners, and real needs.

03

Scalable Impact

We focus on solutions that can grow from a pilot with a few families to platforms serving thousands — without losing the human touch.

Founder

Aliia Bakhtina

Founder & CEO

With deep experience in AI product development and a personal mission rooted in understanding how children learn, grow, and overcome challenges, Aliia founded Futera to bridge the gap between cutting-edge science and practical, life-changing technology.

Her multidisciplinary background enables Futera to approach problems from angles that purely technical or purely academic teams often miss — creating solutions that are both scientifically rigorous and genuinely useful.
